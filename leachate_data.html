Leachate Collection Procedure

  1. Calibrate PCSTestr 35
  2. Power off the lamps and remove the chamber lids to accelerate heat dissipation
  3. Place sample containers underneath the corresponding chambers
  4. Use the misting nozzle to evenly spray water on rock samples
  5. Repeat step 3 for all chambers
  6. After leachate samples are collected, immediately take measurements for EC, TDS and pH
  7. Move the samples to the cold room
